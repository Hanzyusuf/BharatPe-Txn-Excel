Steps:

--- Disable "xpinstall.signatures.required" tag in FireFox.
1. Open about:config: In Firefox, type "about:config" in the address bar and press Enter.
2. Find "xpinstall.signatures.required" and set it to FALSE

--- Install the Extension:
1. Go to about:addons.
2. Click on the hamburger menu (three horizontal lines). 
3. Click on "Install Add-on from File".
4. Select the xpi file of your extension.

Restart Firefox: After installing, you may need to restart Firefox for the changes to take effect.
